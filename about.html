Gianni Jahziel / About Me

This is in the 3rd person because, well, I feel better that way.

Gianni likes to have his hands in a lot of projects. By day, Gianni is an award winning photographer at RoaringSteed who specializes in the concept production and design of engaging online experiences. When not plugging along on new design projects or chasing amazing sunsets, Gianni can be found riding his skateboard or training for his next street marathon.

Prior to his current roles, he started his career as a web designer at Blitz Web Design and has enjoyed transitioning his love of typography and design fundamentals into interactive and emotional online experiences. Gianni also enjoys mentoring in the local design community through educational forum programs at The University of Technology of Jamaica.
